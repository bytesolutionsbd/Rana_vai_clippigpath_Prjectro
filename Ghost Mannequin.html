GHOST MANNEQUIN

Ghost Mannequin or Neck Joint Service is another kind of background removal service from a dummy or mannequin. This is a kind of extraction of the product from dummy and give a still natural look. In this processing a X (logo) file is needed to support back-part editing.

The "ghost mannequin” effect, also known as the "invisible mannequin,” “3D mannequin,” or “hollow man,” is a simple and powerful post-production technique for apparel product photography that solves the showcase problem. The ghost mannequin effect allows you to take multiple photographs of a product on a model or mannequin, and then combine the photos to remove the model or mannequin in post-production processing. The result is a lifelike image that keeps the focus firmly on your product.
